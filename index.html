About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO established on 23 September 2020. The organization works across India to improve lives through education, food distribution, women empowerment, environment protection, animal welfare and skill development. Our mission is to inspire people to create a positive impact in society through volunteerism and community service.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Food distribution, clothes donation and helping underprivileged families.

Project UDAAN

Women empowerment through skill development.

Project JEEV

Care and protection for stray animals.

Project PRAKRITI

Tree plantation and environmental awareness.

Project VIKAS

Internship opportunities and employability skill development.
